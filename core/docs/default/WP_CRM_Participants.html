Class WP_CRM_Participants

Basket is an important class as it emulates a shopping basket and is an ancestor of Invoice.

Basket can hold predefined Products or temporary (meta) one-use products. That's why it has the const MetaSeries attached - to providy meta products with a unique SKU.
